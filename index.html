So, a little about me...

Hi there! My name is Yuan, and I am an international student from New Zealand currently pursuing Business Administration and Computer Science at the University of California, at Berkeley.

On campus, I am a Research Apprentice at the Haas School of Business working under professors Giovani Compiani and Matteo Benetton and a Peer Mentor for the Berkeley International Office. I am also an Academic Intern for core lower-division computer science classes that aims to help students succeed in the subject, and a member of our school badminton team.

I'm deeply interested in technology, and thoroughly enjoy traveling, eating, and sleeping. As I explore my interests, be sure to check back regularly to learn more about my recent developments. In the meantime, take a look at my projects below.
